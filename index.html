How it started

Our story begins in June 2020 at the tail end of the COVID pandemic's first wave. Each of us was isolated in our own social bubbles, but we used the internet to send out our "romance signals." Thanks to a little social networking magic, we connected, and the sparks started to fly. After a few messages, we moved to WhatsApp, where the fun really began. Like two submarines sending out echoes, John would share pictures from his outdoor adventures, while Parisa sent cryptic snapshots from her own escapades. Summer came and went, and we knew we had to meet in person. But self-isolation policies kept us waiting a bit longer, adding to the anticipation.

Finally, the big day came on October 2nd, 2020. Parisa showed up in a bright red fall coat and dressed to impress. John, on the other hand, went for a casual look with baggy jeans and a loose shirt. The evening started with drinks, but we quickly decided the night was still young and moved on to another venue for games and more fun. Five hours later, our first date was one for the history books. It was a night filled with laughter, conversation, and the spark of something special.

From that point on, each date felt like surfing through the "pandemic waves." To spend time together, we often had to travel outside the city to places where stay-at-home policies hadn't taken effect. It was like navigating a maze, dodging restrictions while building our relationship. These trials brought us closer together, and we soon fell in love.

Fast-forward three years, and we had two big reasons to celebrate: Parisa’s birthday and our third anniversary on October 7th, 2023. John surprised Parisa with an engagement ring and asked her to marry him, with both of our parents there to witness the moment. Parisa’s father blessed our engagement with a traditional ceremony, and we all celebrated with delicious food, music, and cake.
